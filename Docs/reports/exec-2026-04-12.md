# Drift Exec Report — 2026-04-12

## What is Drift?
AI-first local health tracker with two equally important pillars: (1) on-device AI chat that handles 90% of interactions — type what you did, AI does the rest, and (2) beautiful, fast health tracking UI for charts, diary, and insights. Privacy-first: everything on-device, no cloud, no accounts. Published on TestFlight.

## AI Chat — Working Examples

| Query | What happens |
|-------|-------------|
| "log 2 eggs and toast for breakfast" | Parses items, opens recipe builder with per-item macros |
| "how's my weight" | Shows 90-day trend, goal progress, weekly comparison |
| "calories in banana" | Instant nutrition lookup from local DB |
| "start push day" | Launches workout from saved template |
| "I weigh 165" | Logs weight, shows trend, goal delta |

## AI Chat — Known Gaps

| Query | Issue |
|-------|-------|
| "plan my meals for today" | Single response instead of iterative suggest → confirm → next meal |
| "build me a PPL split" | No multi-turn workout program builder yet |
| "show me my weight chart" | No navigation-from-chat tool yet |
| Freeform long meal logging | "log breakfast: eggs, toast, coffee with milk and protein powder" — partially working, needs refinement |
